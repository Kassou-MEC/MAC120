When One Goes Up, The Other Goes Down!

Understanding Negative Correlation

A negative correlation means that as one variable increases, the other variable tends to decrease. The correlation coefficient (r) ranges from -1 to 0.

Strong Negative
r = -0.8 to -1.0
Very predictable inverse relationship
Moderate Negative
r = -0.5 to -0.8
Noticeable inverse relationship
Weak Negative
r = -0.1 to -0.5
Slight inverse relationship
